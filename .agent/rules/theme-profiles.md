# Design Archetypes & Theme Profiles

Use these predefined archetypes to jumpstart a high-quality, cohesive design. Each profile defines a specific mood, color strategy, and typography pairing.

## 1. Tech Innovation (Digital Edge)
- **Mood**: High-contrast, forward-thinking, electric.
- **Palette**: Deep Charcoal (#121212), Electric Blue (#0066FF), Neon Cyan (#00FFFF), Pure White (#FFFFFF).
- **Typography**: Bold Sans-Serif (e.g., *Space Grotesk*, *Cabinet Grotesk*) paired with a clean monospace for metadata.
- **Visuals**: Glow effects, subtle grid lines, translucent "glass" panels, high-contrast borders.

## 2. Midnight Galaxy (Cosmic Luxury)
- **Mood**: Deep, mysterious, premium dark mode.
- **Palette**: Obsidian (#050505), Deep Violet (#2E0249), Solar Gold (#F1C40F), Off-White (#F8F9FA).
- **Typography**: Elegant Serif (e.g., *Playfair Display*) paired with a thin, letter-spaced Sans-Serif.
- **Visuals**: Soft radial gradients, mesh backgrounds, aura glows, ultra-thin borders.

## 3. Nordic Frost (Minimalist Clean)
- **Mood**: Crisp, professional, airy, focused.
- **Palette**: Snow (#FFFFFF), Ice Blue (#F0F4F8), Deep Slate (#1A365D), Arctic Gray (#E2E8F0).
- **Typography**: Geometric Sans-Serif (e.g., *Outfit*, *Plus Jakarta Sans*) with consistent weight distribution.
- **Visuals**: Heavy whitespace, soft shadows, rounded corners (large radius), clean separators.

## 4. Desert Rose (Sophisticated Warmth)
- **Mood**: Earthy, soft, editorial, approachable.
- **Palette**: Sand (#F3E5D8), Dusky Rose (#D4A373), Terracotta (#BC6C25), Dark Umber (#283618).
- **Typography**: Characterful Serif (e.g., *Bricolage Grotesque*, *Fraunces*) paired with a soft geometric Sans.
- **Visuals**: Grain textures, organic shapes, overlapping elements, subtle paper-like background.

## 5. Industrial Utilitarian (The Blueprint)
- **Mood**: Raw, functional, technical, high-density.
- **Palette**: Blueprint Blue (#003366), Safety Orange (#FF8C00), Concrete Gray (#D1D5DB), Pitch Black (#000000).
- **Typography**: Monospace (e.g., *JetBrains Mono*) for everything, playing with scale and weight for hierarchy.
- **Visuals**: Exposed grid lines, data-heavy tables, technical callouts, high-contrast borders (2px+).

## 6. Forest Canopy (Eco-Organic)
- **Mood**: Grounded, natural, calm, sustainable.
- **Palette**: Deep Moss (#1A2E05), Fern Green (#4F772D), Eucalyptus (#90A955), Cream (#ECF39E).
- **Typography**: Humanist Sans (e.g., *Syne*) paired with a friendly, readable body font.
- **Visuals**: Bionic curves, leaf-shaped masks, soft green tints, blurred natural imagery.

## Implementation Guide
1. **Choose an Archetype**: Match the archetype to the brand or project context.
2. **Setup CSS Variables**: Map these colors to your `--primary`, `--secondary`, `--background` variables.
3. **Respect the Mood**: Don't mix "Industrial" visuals with "Nordic Frost" colors. Stay consistent within the archetype.
4. **Iterate**: Use these as a foundation, then modify 10-20% to make it unique to the project.
